A Bit About Us

Bloom Mind was founded in February 2025 by a group of passionate students and mental health advocates. It began as a small project on the school premises of Rosebank College, Braamfontein, aiming to help peers track their moods and connect with trustworthy people without stigma or judgment. What started as casual peer check-ins soon grew into a safe, supportive network for students from all walks of life. Today, Bloom Mind continues to create a space where openness about mental health is encouraged, and where every student feels understood, valued, and supported in their journey toward wellness.

Mission Statement

To create a world where no student feels alone in their struggles where caring for mental health is as normal, safe, and stigma-free as asking for academic help. We believe in providing judgment-free spaces, accessible support, and genuine community so students can find the help they need and thrive in both mind and spirit.

Vision Statement

Our vision is to be the leading student-focused mental health network in South Africa, empowering young people to take control of their wellbeing without fear or shame. We aim to foster a generation where mental health care is integrated into everyday student life, celebrated as an act of strength, and supported by compassionate communities across all campuses.
